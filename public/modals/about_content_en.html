About StudyWithMe

Video Study Trainer (StudyWithMe) is an AI study coaching service that helps you stay focused at home while watching YouTube study-with-me videos.

Your feedback helps us improve StudyWithMe.

Key Features

  • AI Focus & Stability Analysis: Analyzes your movement in real-time and provides focus scores.
  • YouTube Integration: Search for any study-with-me video or add one via URL to follow along.
  • Privacy Protection: Video data is processed only in your browser and never sent to a server.
  • Playlist Management: Save and manage your favorite study videos.
  • Environment Optimization: Adjust opacity, sensitivity, and playback speed in the settings.

How to Use

  1. Select Video: Choose a study-with-me video from the playlist or search on YouTube.
  2. Allow Camera Access: Please allow camera access for AI analysis.
  3. Start Studying: Study along with the video, and the AI will show your real-time SCORE.
  4. Adjust Settings: Optimize your environment by adjusting opacity, sensitivity, and playback speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to sign up?

A: No, StudyWithMe can be used immediately without any sign-up. All data is stored in your browser.

Q: I denied camera permission. What should I do?

A: Click the camera icon next to the browser address bar → Change to "Allow" → Refresh the page.

Q: Do I need a YouTube API key?

A: No, the web version handles search functionality automatically. You don't need to provide your own API key. If search isn't working, it might be a temporary network issue.

Q: What kind of study videos do you recommend?

A: We recommend study-with-me videos where your upper body and desk are clearly visible. Timers, ambient sound, or quiet study sessions work best.

Q: Can I use it on a smartphone?

A: Yes, it works normally on mobile browsers. However, we recommend a PC environment as the screen is larger.

Q: How is the score calculated?

A: The AI detects your movement through the camera. The more you minimize movement and stay focused in a good posture, the higher your SCORE will be.

Q: Is my personal information safe?

A: All video processing takes place only within the browser and is not sent to a server. Complete privacy protection is guaranteed.

Q: Is it free?

A: Yes, StudyWithMe is a completely free service. It is operated through ad revenue.

Q: Can I use it offline?

A: An internet connection is required to play YouTube videos. AI processing is done locally.

Q: Which browsers are supported?

A: We support the latest web browsers such as Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Chrome is highly recommended.

Guide for Effective Studying

🎥 Camera Setup

  • Position: Place the camera so your upper body and desk fit in the frame.
  • Distance: A distance of 1-2 meters from the camera is appropriate.
  • Height: Installing the camera at chest or eye level allows for the most accurate recognition.
  • Lighting: Studying under bright lighting improves AI recognition rates.

📌 Study Posture Tips

  • Face Front: Keep your upper body facing the camera.
  • Go Slow: At first, learn the flow slowly at 0.75x speed.
  • Adjust Opacity: Compare your posture with the person in the video by adjusting the camera brightness.
  • Stay Focused: Minimize your movement and immerse yourself in study while watching your real-time SCORE.

🎯 How to Improve Your SCORE

  • Adjust Sensitivity: Adjust the motion detection intensity with the sensitivity slider.
  • Face Visibility: Postures where your face and upper body are clearly visible are best for scoring.
  • Clear Background: Studying against a solid-colored background increases AI recognition rates.

📋 Recommended Study Routines

  • Beginner: 25 min focus + 5 min break (1 Pomodoro)
  • Intermediate: 50 min focus + 10 min break + 20 min review
  • Advanced: 90 min focus + 15 min break + 30 min problem-solving

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

StudyWithMe collects the following information:

  • Playlist Data: List of videos saved by the user (browser localStorage)
  • Settings Information: User settings such as opacity, sensitivity, and playback speed
  • Search Queries: Search terms entered by the user

2. Camera Video Processing

IMPORTANT: Video captured by the camera is NOT sent to a server.

  • All video processing takes place only in the browser (client).
  • AI analysis is performed locally through the on-device engine.
  • No video data is stored or transmitted externally.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored only in the browser's localStorage:

  • Playlist information
  • User settings (opacity, sensitivity, etc.)

If you delete browser data, all information will be deleted as well.

4. Use of YouTube API

The YouTube video search function uses Google YouTube Data API v3:

  • API keys for search are managed securely by the service and not exposed to users.
  • Only search queries are sent to YouTube, and no personal user info is transmitted.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

StudyWithMe does not use cookies directly and only uses localStorage. However, cookies may be used in the following cases:

  • When displaying Google AdSense ads (advertising cookies)
  • When playing YouTube videos (YouTube cookies)
